{...About}

  • Context Coding is an app development program that aims to minimize the knowledge gap between experienced developers and brand new programmers by providing a mentorship-type of approach to education.
  • I provide full stack software development coaching to those with little or no experience.
  • I give new devs context of where to get started, how to build complex web applications, and I help my students pursue opportunities for employment.
